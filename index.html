About Shine

Shine is a simple and powerful tool that helps users find and help children in need. Our advanced location-based functionality makes it easy to identify and assist children who are deprived of the essentials or who need care. By partnering with Food For Charity, we are able to make a tangible impact in the lives of children.

Features of Shine

  • Advanced location-based functionality to identify and assist children in need
  • Easy-to-use interface for identifying and helping children in need
  • Partnership with Food For Charity to provide food for children in need
